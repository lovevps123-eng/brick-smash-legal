# Privacy Policy for Brick Smash

**Effective Date:** June 16, 2026
**Last Updated:** June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Brick Smash ("we", "us", "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application. We have designed Brick Smash to collect as little data as possible — most game data stays on your device.

---

## 1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Brick Smash does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- Your name, email, phone number, or any personal contact information
- Account credentials (we have no user accounts or login system)
- Your photos, contacts, calendar, location, or microphone data
- Any health data
- Any biometric data
- Payment card details (purchases are handled entirely by Apple)

---

## 2. Information Stored On-Device Only

The following data is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's standard UserDefaults / iCloud sync:

- Your high scores and game history
- Your selected theme (Classic, Neon, Pixel, OLED Dark)
- Your sound and haptic preferences
- Whether you have purchased the "Remove Ads" option

This data **never leaves your device** (except optionally synced via iCloud per Apple's standard policies, which we do not control or access).

---

## 3. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Brick Smash is free to play, supported by advertising. We use the following third-party services to serve ads and process purchases. Each service has its own privacy policy:

### 3.1 AppLovin MAX (Ad Mediation)
- **Purpose:** Selects which ad network to show ads from
- **Data they may collect:** Device advertising identifier (IDFA — only if you grant permission), IP address, ad interaction events, approximate location (country/region)
- **Privacy Policy:** https://www.applovin.com/privacy/

### 3.2 Pangle (Ad Network — Operated by ByteDance)
- **Purpose:** Serves video ads on behalf of advertisers
- **Data they may collect:** Device advertising identifier (IDFA — only if granted), IP address, ad interaction events
- **Privacy Policy:** https://www.pangleglobal.com/privacy

### 3.3 Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchase)
- **Purpose:** Processes the optional one-time "Remove Ads" purchase ($2.99)
- **Data Apple may collect:** Purchase receipt, Apple ID (Apple's responsibility, not ours)
- **Privacy Policy:** https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### 3.4 Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT)
On first significant use of the app, iOS will show a system dialog asking whether you grant permission for ad personalization. You can:
- **Allow:** AppLovin and Pangle may use your IDFA to show more relevant ads (does not improve game functionality)
- **Ask Not To Track:** Ads will still be shown but will not be personalized
- **Change anytime:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Brick Smash

If you "Ask Not To Track", no IDFA is shared with any third party.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Brick Smash is rated **17+** because it serves third-party advertisements. The app is **not** designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us at the email below and we will investigate.

---

## 5. Your Rights

You can exercise the following rights at any time:

- **View what's stored locally:** Open Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Brick Smash. All game data is on your device.
- **Delete your data:** Uninstall the app. All local data is deleted with the uninstall.
- **Opt out of ad personalization:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Brick Smash → Off
- **Request information about ad-network data:** Contact AppLovin or Pangle directly via the links in Section 3.

We do not have user accounts and therefore cannot delete server-side data for you (because we don't have any).

---

## 6. International Users

Brick Smash is available worldwide. If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California:

- **GDPR / UK GDPR:** Our legal basis for processing limited data via third-party ad networks is *legitimate interest* (to keep the app free). You may opt out via ATT (Section 3.4).
- **CCPA:** We do not sell personal information. The ad-personalization opt-out (ATT) covers California's "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" right.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Third-party ad networks:** See their respective privacy policies.
- **Apple purchase records:** Subject to Apple's standard retention policy.

We retain no data ourselves, so there is no data for us to delete.
